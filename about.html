WELCOME TO
PLYO FIT

Mr VIPIN O V is a veteran soldier from INDIAN ARMED FORCES. Vipin served Indian air force for long 15 years as sports person and fitness trainer. Being a school boy Vipin was a lean and short student compared his friends and they make fun of him and often laughed at calling him as CHOTTU (smallest kid). This provoked him decided to build good and attractive physique, hence he started exercising , playing football etc and at the age of seventeen he joint military to pursue his fitness and adventurous passion. Later at his twenties he banged medals in AIRFORCE SERVICES and SENIOR NATIONAL body building championships. HE IS REALLY A TRUE MoTIVAToR AND ROLE MODEL FOR YOUNGSTERS. and also very passionate in sports fitness and continued his education in health , fitness and physical education sector . He qualified Kerala government Teacher Eligibility Test ( K TET ) also. He is a qualified tutor and assessor for international fitness trainer certifications. He started his dream startup PLYO FIT in 2017. he selected the firm name PLYO FIT by himself (which means plyo - increase in Latin & fit -fitness ). His aim is to increase the fitness of society by creating energetic and qualified professional trainers. So he kept PLYO FIT Slogan as

MISSSION

At Plyo Fit Fitness Trainer Course Center, our mission is to empower aspiring fitness trainers with extensive education, practical training, and unwavering support. We are committed to equipping students with the knowledge, skills and confidence needed to excel in fitness coaching. Through a holistic approach to education, hands-on training sessions and personalized support, we strive to foster a supportive learning environment where students can thrive and succeed. Our ultimate goal is to empower future fitness leaders who are prepared to make a positive impact in the lives of others, promoting health wellness, and happiness through fitness coaching. Turn fat in to fit

VISION

Plyo Fit Fitness Trainer Course Center envisions becoming the premier destination for aspiring fitness professionals, renowned for excellence in education, innovation in training methodologies, and commitment to student success. We aim to create a vibrant learning community where students are inspired to pursue their passion for fitness, equipped with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to thrive in the industry. Our vision is to empower individuals to transform lives through fitness coaching, fostering a healthier, happier world one client at a time.
